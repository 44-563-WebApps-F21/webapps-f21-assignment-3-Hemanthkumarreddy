All for one

I was born into a world where nations compete for supremacy with their superpowers. As a result, I was one of them. My superpowers consist of appropriating other people's superpowers and claiming them as my own. I have a variety of superpowers, like airstroke, splitting, teleportation, and the ability to spew both ice and fire at the same time, among others. The more extraordinary powers I obtain, the worse my health becomes. For the time being, I'm looking for a superpower that will restore my damaged cells and allow me to be the most powerful person on this planet.
